About me

Senior Product Designer
with a systems mindset.

I'm Or Noga, a Senior Product Designer with 6 years of experience, including 5 years leading design as a senior designer and team lead, and currently as a solo product design lead. I specialize in end-to-end design: from UX research and discovery through high-fidelity UI, design systems, and developer handoff.

I've led design across multiple industries and complex technical domains, always working closely with PMs and engineers to translate intricate requirements into experiences that feel clear and trustworthy to the people using them. I actively integrate AI tools into my workflow, using them to accelerate research synthesis, generate and pressure-test design concepts, and ship faster without compromising quality.
