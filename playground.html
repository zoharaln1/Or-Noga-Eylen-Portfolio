Selected Projects

Four products.
Four different worlds.

Between 2020 and 2024 at PitangoUX, I designed interfaces for a wide range of products — each with its own users, constraints, and visual language. Here's a focused look at the UI side of that work.

01
Routines

A versatile Workload Automation and Job Scheduling platform designed to streamline and simplify complex business processes. With an agile marketplace of automation methods, Routines centralizes backend scheduling for multi-step tasks — available on both cloud and on-premise platforms.

B2B SaaS Automation Dashboard Design
02
EasyShul

A comprehensive cloud-based software platform for synagogue management. Features modules for accounting, fundraising, event management, education, and member self-service — combining administrative power with a clean, accessible interface.

Non-profit Tech Cloud Platform Admin UI
03
Tetavi

A startup specializing in photorealistic digital content and experiences for the Metaverse and Web3. Tetavi uses advanced volumetric technology to bring real humans into the digital world — enabling immersive content creation at the intersection of tech and art.

Metaverse Web3 Creative Tech
04
SpringGames

A mobile game development company specializing in interactive multiplayer online games. SpringGames merges virtual adventures with real-life rewards — designing for high engagement, delight, and a uniquely gamified experience across platforms.

Mobile Gaming Multiplayer Gamification
